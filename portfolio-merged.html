Learning Phase: First Rodeo with Paid Ads

First major campaign as CMO. Goal: 2,000 student enrollments by Dec 31st deadline. Reality: missed target significantly but gained critical learnings. Complex multi-campaign funnel, feature-listing creative, micro-segmented audiences. Result: 7.05x ROAS (slight improvement over 6.05x baseline), but proved paid marketing viability.
‚ùå What Failed
Feature-Listing Creative
Generic carousel posts listing product features performed terribly. <1% CTR. Students didn't care about features; they wanted solutions to problems.
Complex Funnel Over-Engineering
Built 10+ ad sets trying to segment micro-audiences. Result: split budgets too thin, Meta algorithm couldn't optimize effectively, high CPMs.
Late Campaign Launch
Launched too close to deadline (mid-Dec for Dec 31 goal). Not enough time to test, learn, and iterate. Deadline-driven audience behavior meant we missed peak enrollment window.
‚úì What Worked
Proved Paid Ads Viability
7.05x ROAS beat baseline (6.05x). Demonstrated that with optimization, paid marketing could be profitable for SIMAKARA.
Audience Insights
Learned target audience exhibits deadline-driven behavior. Students enroll close to program start dates. Need to launch campaigns earlier.

‚ùå What Changed

Methodology

1 Channel Focus

Meta Ads only allocation. Tight budgets demand focus. Proven ROI on Meta meant strategic decision to deepen expertise rather than spread capital across untested channels. Mastery > Diversification in resource-constrained environments.

2 Targeting Strategy

Broad targeting signals > complex micro-funnels. Algorithmic optimization proved superior to manual segmentation (40%+ performance improvement). Trust Meta's algorithm; avoid over-engineering. Creative differentiation beats audience targeting in Meta 2025.

3 Messaging Framework

Solution-focused positioning: Problem ‚Üí Your Approach ‚Üí Proof. Address systemic pain points (not product features). Build credibility through understanding, not hard-sell. Segmented messaging for different audience contexts (Gap Year vs Kelas 12).

4 Content Format

Authentic tutor demos + editorial storytelling. Relatability over polish. Real teachers teaching. Show > Tell. Poster format for premium positioning. Weekender-style narratives addressing systemic problems without being salesy.

5 Tracking & Attribution

Meta Pixel + GA4 double-tracking. Single-source attribution has blind spots. Dual verification improves confidence in ROAS and enables better budget allocation decisions. Implemented mid-Campaign #3 when realized limitations.

6 Budget Philosophy

Efficiency over volume. Work under constraints. Proof: 73% less budget, better ROAS (13x ‚Üí 73x). Strategic optimization scales better than budget increases. Constraint forces prioritization and peak efficiency.

7 Sequential Funnel

Traffic campaigns build retargeting pools at ultra-low cost (Rp 490/LPV), then conversion campaigns target warm audience. Pool-building strategy was game changer for Campaign #3 efficiency.

8 Compound Learning

Each campaign built on previous insights. Learning compounds; budget doesn't. Campaign #3 success came from applying lessons from #1 & #2. Systematic experimentation > starting fresh each time.
